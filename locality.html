Discover the Locality

Things to do

  • West Cork Equine Centre: (087) 271 7008
    The West Cork Equine Centre is a family-run equestrian facility that's open all-year round and caters for all equestrian disciplines.
  • West Cork Secret: (023) 884 9606/(087) 833 1206
    The West Cork Secret is a magical hideaway, loved by all who discover it. It couples the serenity of beautifully landscaped gardens with the shrieks of excitement from the obstacle course.

Beaches

  • Harbour View: A large beautiful beach at the mouth of Courtmacsherry bay & Kilbrittain estuary popular with swimmers, walkers, wind-surfers and kite-surfers.
    View map location →
  • Coolmain Beach: A gorgeous sheltered little beach on the east side of Kilbrittain estuary with shallow calmer waters, great for swimming.
    View map location →
  • Howe Strand: A beautiful and secluded beach overlooked by the ruins of an old coastguard station — great for swimming, surfing, body-boarding and fishing from the rocks.
    View map location →
